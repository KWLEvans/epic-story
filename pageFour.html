The Finale

Spying the store on the horizon, the cobbler felt his waning spirits rise. After a lifetime of searching, he had finally found it. But in the eternal race toward death, dogs always run faster and it seemed Dogdog had run his course. The man began to run, shouting, "We did it Dogdog; we finally made it." It wasn't until he had nearly arrived that he realized the steading panting of his eternal companion was no more...

He turned, seeing the hollow shell of his once dearest friend. "We did it," he sobbed, "I always knew we'd make it Dogdog." He held his sweet, sweet pal one last time and breathed... his... final... breath...
